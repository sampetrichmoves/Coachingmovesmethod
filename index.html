What is a Pain Lane?
Muscle — Muscle/tendon causing pain. Dull ache, gets worse with load.
Joint — Joint/cartilage/disc. Pinching, catching, position-specific.
Nerve — Nerve irritated. Electric, burning, radiating sensation.
Diagnostics in Week 1 identify which lane. Each lane gets different exercise modifications.
Progress only when: Pain ↓ · Movement easier
